Zombified Apple Pie

Apple pie, the first of its kind !

Just make sure to handle all of these ingredients with care, as they may induce vomiting, diarrhea, or even zombification. Enjoy! (Or maybe don't.)

Ingredients

  • Rotten apples (the more mold, the better)
  • Spoiled milk (for that extra tangy flavor)
  • Maggot-infested flour (adds a nice crunch)
  • Blood of the undead (gives it that lovely red color)
  • Dirt (for that earthy undertone)
  • Rusty nails (adds a touch of metal to the mix)
  • Zombie brain matter (because why not?)
  • Moldy bread crumbs (for the perfect crust)
  • Decaying cinnamon (gives it that zesty kick)
  • Acidic lemon juice (to really bring out the sour notes)

Recipe instructions (taken from the Necronomicon)

  1. First, preheat your oven to 375°F, because even zombie apple pies need a good baking.
  2. Next, start by peeling and coring your rotten apples. Be sure to leave as much mold on as possible, because that's where all the flavor is!
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together your maggot-infested flour and moldy bread crumbs. This will form the perfect crust for your zombie apple pie.
  4. Now it's time to add some tangy flavor to your pie filling. Pour in a healthy dose of spoiled milk, and mix until it's nice and lumpy.
  5. To really make your zombie apple pie stand out, add in some blood of the undead. This will give it a lovely red color that's perfect for Halloween parties.
  6. For that earthy undertone, sprinkle in a little dirt. Don't worry, it's perfectly safe to eat as long as you don't mind a little grit in your teeth.
  7. For an extra kick, add in some rusty nails. Not only do they add a touch of metal to the mix, but they're a great source of iron too!
  8. Now it's time for the pièce de résistance: zombie brain matter. Because why not? Just be sure to chop it up into small pieces so it's evenly distributed throughout the pie.
  9. Finally, sprinkle in some decaying cinnamon and a squeeze of acidic lemon juice. This will really bring out the sour notes and give your pie that extra zesty kick.
  10. Once your pie filling is complete, pour it into your prepared crust and pop it in the oven for 45 minutes. And voila! You now have a deliciously gruesome zombie apple pie to share with all your friends and family.
