BlueBerry Pie

BlueBerry Pie? I shudder of the thought of it!

This blueberry pie is sweet and tempting, with its plump and juicy blueberries bursting with flavor. However, what you don't realize is that hidden in the pie are toxic ingredients that will have you feeling ill in no time. One bite of this pie and you'll be regretting your decision, as the poison courses through your body. So be warned, this blueberry pie is a deadly dessert that you should avoid at all costs.

Ingredients

  • Poisonous blueberries (the more toxic, the better)
  • Rotten eggs (for that extra sulfurous smell)
  • Cyanide-laced sugar (for a deadly sweet taste)
  • Toxic waste (for that neon blue color)
  • Moldy butter (adds a touch of green)
  • Arsenic powder (for that metallic tang)
  • Radioactive flour (to give it that extra zing)
  • Rat droppings (because why not?)
  • Deadly nightshade (for a hint of bitterness)
  • Plutonium sprinkles (just for fun)

This pie is definitely not for the faint of heart or stomach. Please do not attempt to eat it, or even make it, unless you want to experience some serious health risks!
