Ingredients

  • Fresh lemons (for that zesty kick)
  • Sweetened condensed milk (for a creamy texture)
  • Flaky pie crust (for that perfect crunch)
  • Vanilla extract (for a sweet aroma)
  • Granulated sugar (to balance out the tartness of the lemons)
  • Egg yolks (for binding and richness)
  • Cornstarch (to thicken the filling)
  • Whipped egg whites (for a fluffy meringue)
  • Cream of tartar (to stabilize the meringue)
  • Love and care (because every good pie needs a little bit of that)

Recipe instructions (taken from the book of creation)

  1. Preheat your oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Roll out your pie crust and place it into a pie dish.
  3. Prick the crust with a fork and bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes or until lightly golden.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together your condensed milk, lemon juice, lemon zest, egg yolks, and cornstarch until well combined.
  5. Pour the mixture into the partially baked pie crust and bake for an additional 12-15 minutes or until the filling is set.
  6. While the pie is baking, make the meringue by beating the egg whites and cream of tartar until soft peaks form.
  7. Add in the sugar and continue beating until stiff peaks form.
  8. Remove the pie from the oven and spread the meringue on top of the filling.
  9. Bake for an additional 10-12 minutes or until the meringue is lightly browned.
  10. Let it cool before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
